The Company

Computer World is one of the world’s best electrical retailers, with a million people and employees connected worldwide. Computer World sells a multitude of products ranging from laptops, monitors, graphics cards and other peripherals. The company's warehouse is located in Southall, from where over 20,000 weekly orders are delivered to customers throughout the UK.

We provide the best quality products to customers, and our delivery services are expanding day by day. Customers can be assured that we provide the best quality at a competitive price. We also have a Pre-built PC division for customers for require a PC without the hassle.

Our Service

Computer World’s primary focus is to help customers and save them time. If customers have any issues with any product, our customer service number is on the website. They can call at any time during working hours of 9-6PM, Mon-Fri. When customers purchase any product, they are sent a confirmation email. They must keep this for any potential returns/warranty claims.

We are always looking to improve our service to customers, and this means listening to what our customers want. If you have any feedback for us, we look forward to hearing from you.
